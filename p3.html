• Indian Psychiatric Society (IPS)

    IPS is one of the largest professional bodies of psychiatrists in India. It focuses on promoting mental health, advancing the knowledge and skills of psychiatrists, and advocating for mental health policies. The IPS conducts research, organizes conferences, and provides educational resources for mental health professionals.

  • National Academy of Medical Sciences (NAMS)

    NAMS is an autonomous institution that works toward promoting and advancing medical and allied sciences in India. While not exclusively focused on psychiatry, it may include mental health-related research, education, and advocacy among its activities.

  • Indian Association of Clinical Psychologists (IACP)

    IACP is dedicated to the development and advancement of clinical psychology in India. It focuses on professional networking, education, training, and research in the field of clinical psychology. This organization often collaborates with mental health institutions and professionals to promote best practices.

  • Indian Association of Social Psychiatry (IASP)

    IASP aims to promote mental health from a social perspective. It focuses on understanding and addressing the social determinants of mental health, advocating for social policies that support mental well-being, and fostering collaboration between psychiatry and social sciences.

  • Indian Association of Private Psychiatry (IAPP)

    IAPP is an organization of private psychiatrists in India. It works to promote high-quality mental health care in private practice settings, supports professional development, and advocates for the interests of private practitioners.

  • Indian Association for Geriatric Mental Health (IAGMH)

    IAGMH focuses on mental health issues related to aging and elderly populations in India. It aims to improve mental health services, conduct research, and raise awareness about the mental health needs of older adults.

  • Indian Association for Child and Adolescent Mental Health (IACAM)

    IACAM is dedicated to addressing mental health issues among children and adolescents. It works to promote awareness, provide resources, and support professionals working in child and adolescent mental health.

  • Association of Medical Consultants (AMC)

    While not exclusively focused on mental health, AMC represents medical consultants across various specialties, including psychiatry. It may work toward advocating for healthcare policies, providing professional development opportunities, and fostering collaboration among medical professionals.
